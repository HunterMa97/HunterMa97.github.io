Hi, I'm Haotian, a final year Ph.D. student in [Computer Science](https://www.cs.wisc.edu/) at the [University of Wisconsin-Madison](https://www.wisc.edu/), where I am fortunate to be advised by [Prof. Daifeng Wang](https://daifengwanglab.org/people/). Before joining Daifeng's Lab, I had a wonderful time with [Prof. Aws Albarghouthi](https://pages.cs.wisc.edu/~aws/) and [Prof. Frederic Sala](https://pages.cs.wisc.edu/~fredsala/). I received my bachelor's degree from [SUSTech](https://sustech.edu.cn/en/) in 2021, supervised by [Prof. Hu Xu](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=pE1e0rsAAAAJ&hl=en). Before that, I had a wonderful internship at the [John Hopcroft Center](https://jhc.sjtu.edu.cn/), where I learned a lot from [Prof. Quanshi Zhang](https://sjtu-xai-lab.github.io/).

My research focuses on Explainable AI in the real world, with the goal of transforming deep learning from a "black box" into a reliable tool. To this end, I develop interpretable frameworks spanning deep learning theory and broader applications.

I am actively looking for positions related to LLM safety and interpretability. I also welcome review invitations from AI conferences, journals, and workshops as a way of giving back to this growing community.

I enjoy talking with people and working together. If you have an idea you would like to share, or are looking for potential ways to collaborate, feel free to reach out!

## Education

- **Southern University of Science and Technology (SUSTech)**, B.S. in Physics, 2016-2021.

## Life

Outside of research, I enjoy writing, game design, and watching TV series. After I graduate, I will look for ways to share it, perhaps through self-publishing, a visual novel, or AI-generated video (hopefully it will be much cheaper by then :D).
